An open-source continuation of Hide My Applist project with bunch of new features.

Features of HMA-OSS:
* Hide your application list (similar to what Hide My Applist does)
* Activity launch protection
* Installation source spoofing for both user and system apps (replaces BetterKnownInstalled)
* Application presets
* Settings presets
* Settings templates (more flexible than what SettingsFirewall does, also allows you to override the settings presets)
* Propless appdata and vold appdata isolation with exclude system apps option
* Ignore hiding an app from Google Play services automatically if it is risky to hide (fixes crash issues etc.)
* Experimental Restrict Zygote permissions option
* Extra opposite app list option to add:
  - More visible apps in blacklist mode
  - More invisible apps in whitelist mode
* Redesigned home screen and some other screens

For more information, check this wiki page: https://github.com/frknkrc44/HMA-OSS/wiki
